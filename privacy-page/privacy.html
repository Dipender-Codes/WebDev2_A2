Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to GoFundMe! Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform to support fundraisers or start one yourself. We are committed to ensuring your personal data is handled in compliance with relevant data protection laws.

By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of your information as outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Collecting name, email address, contact information, and payment details when you create an account, start a fundraiser, or donate.
  • Fundraiser Data: Gathering details about the causes you support or create, including descriptions, images, and funding goals.
  • Usage Data: Capturing information about how you interact with our site, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and duration of your visits.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: Managing your account, authenticating users, and handling donations.
  • Fundraising Activities: Displaying fundraiser information and providing tools for campaign management.
  • Payment Processing: Securely processing donations and distributing funds to the intended recipients.
  • Communication: Sending notifications, updates, and marketing materials (with your consent).
  • Site Improvement: Analyzing user behavior and improving site functionality, security, and user experience.

Sharing Your Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal data. However, we may share your information under the following circumstances:

  • Fundraiser Visibility: Making information about the fundraisers you support or create (excluding payment details) visible to other users and the public.
  • Service Providers: Sharing data with third-party providers for payment processing, email services, and site analytics.
  • Legal Compliance: Disclosing your information when required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our users, the public, or our platform.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our platform uses encryption and other security protocols to ensure your data is safe during transmission and storage.

Despite our best efforts, no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to safeguard your login credentials and contact us if you suspect any security breach.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website:

  • Necessary Cookies: Essential for site functionality, such as enabling login and payment processing.
  • Analytics Cookies: Using cookies to analyze site usage and improve performance.
  • Marketing Cookies: Displaying relevant advertisements to users.

You can modify your browser settings to refuse cookies, but this may limit certain functionalities of the website.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Requesting a copy of your personal data that we hold.
  • Correction: Requesting correction of any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Requesting deletion of your data, subject to legal and operational constraints.
  • Opt-Out: Opting out of receiving promotional communications.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our support team at support@gofundme.com.

Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with the updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page regularly to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
